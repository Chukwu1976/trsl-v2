ABOUT US

Welcome to Trust Rising Sun Limited

your trusted partner in wine distribution. We are passionate about bringing exceptional wines from around the world to retailers, restaurants, and wine enthusiasts across Africa.

At Trust Rising Sun, we believe that wine is more than just a beverage; it is an experience that brings people together, creates memories, and enhances moments of joy and celebration. With this belief at the core of our operations, we strive to provide a diverse selection of high-quality wines that cater to every taste and occasion.

Our team is composed of dedicated wine enthusiasts and industry professionals who are committed to curating a portfolio of wines that represents the best of the wine world. We work closely with renowned vineyards and esteemed winemakers to ensure that each bottle we distribute embodies the unique characteristics and craftsmanship of its origin.

What sets Trust Rising Sun apart is not just our exceptional selection of wines, but also our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We value the relationships we build with our winery partners and our clients, and we take pride in providing personalized service, expert advice, and reliable support every step of the way.

Whether you are a retailer seeking to enhance your wine offerings, a restaurant looking to elevate your wine program, or a wine lover in search of new discoveries, Trust Rising Sun is here to serve you. We understand the diverse needs of our customers and are dedicated to providing tailored solutions that exceed expectations

As a company, we embrace integrity, professionalism, and a continuous pursuit of excellence. We are passionate about the world of wines, and we strive to foster a culture of appreciation, education, and enjoyment. Through our dedication to quality and service, we aim to contribute to the growth and enrichment of the wine industry in Africa.

We invite you to explore our website and discover the exceptional wines we offer. Join us on this exciting journey as we bring the world of wines to your doorstep. Trust in our expertise, experience the rising sun of quality and service, and let us be your trusted partner in all things wine.
