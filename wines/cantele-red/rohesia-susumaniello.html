Rohesia Susumaniello

From medieval Latin, the name of this wine carries its colour, the chromatic symbolism of its masculine and feminine nature, its delicate ambivalence.

From the single rose, unrepeatable each time, to the elegance that connotes the uniqueness of an entire floral species, a different message travels each time. And that message is not just a matter of colour, it is rather a kind of vocation, just like that of the midday light which – depending on the time of day – degrades the volumes of its shades. And here is a polychromy of the passions. These passions make up the Rohesia line.

A triptych. Red. White. Rose. Three colors of the same rose blossomed in the narrative power of the Rohesia line in three possible variations.

The red rose speaks to us of a love, which is a wine, capable every time like the first time of closing our eyes with a kiss. That red poured into the glass is the irreversible heart of Susumaniello that lets itself be tasted while he kidnaps us to take us into the deep pleasure that we have known how to choose.

Tasting notes

COLOR : Intense ruby ​​red, with soft purplish reflections.

NOSE : Bouquet composed of red fruits, raspberries and plums. Also perfectly identifiable are cinnamon, black cherry, red rose petals and, in the finish, white pepper.

PALATE : The taste is characterized by a good gustatory balance between softness and alcohol. Wine with soft tannins, good freshness and great drinkability.
