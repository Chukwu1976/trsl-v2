Rohesia Negroamaro

From medieval Latin, the name of this wine carries its colour, the chromatic symbolism of its masculine and feminine nature, its delicate ambivalence.

From the single rose, unrepeatable each time, to the elegance that connotes the uniqueness of an entire floral species, a different message travels each time. And that message is not just a matter of colour, it is rather a kind of vocation, just like that of the midday light which – depending on the time of day – degrades the volumes of its shades. And here is a polychromy of the passions. These passions make up the Rohesia line.

A triptych. Red. White. Rose. Three colors of the same rose blossomed in the narrative power of the Rohesia line in three possible variations.

There is the rose that remains suspended halfway between the red and the white, therefore the rose, therefore the Negroamaro Rosato is a reverberation that you can still recognize because it is as faithful as certain returns home after a long journey or a collection of distances that , in the end, they give us back the taste of things destined to last because they are the most sincere.

CLASSIFICATION
PGI Puglia
GRAPES
Negroamaro
FARM
Cordon pruned espalier
TIME OF HARVEST
Mid September
MACERATION
Up to about 24 hours
VINIFICATION
Fermentation in steel at 15-16°C
REFINEMENT
Sur lies for about 3 months
AGING
To drink immediately or keep for 2-3 years

Tasting notes

COLOR : Intense cherry pink.

NOSE : Intense and persistent. Hints of red berried fruit, strawberry, peach, cherry. Floral of pink rose and violet. Complex nose, in which flint also appears.

PALATE : Round, elegant, haughty. A wine of remarkable taste-olfactory persistence. Powerful but with great freshness and sapidity.
