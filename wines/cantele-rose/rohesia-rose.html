Rohesia Rosé Pas Dosé

From medieval Latin, the name of this wine carries its colour, the chromatic symbolism of its masculine and feminine nature, its delicate ambivalence.

From the single rose, unrepeatable each time, to the elegance that connotes the uniqueness of an entire floral species, a different message travels each time. And that message is not just a matter of colour, it is rather a kind of vocation, just like that of the midday light which – depending on the time of day – degrades the volumes of its shades. And here is a polychromy of the passions. These passions make up the Rohesia line.

A triptych. Red. White. Rose. Three colors of the same rose blossomed in the narrative power of the Rohesia line in three possible variations.

There is the rose that remains suspended halfway between the red and the white, therefore the rose, therefore the Negroamaro Rosato Pas Dosè is a reverberation that you can still recognize because it is as faithful as certain returns home after a long journey or a collection of distances that, in the end, give us back the taste for things destined to last because they are the most sincere.

Tasting notes

EYE : abundant foam with fine, elegant and persistent perlage.

COLOR : antique pink with coppery reflections that tend towards coral.

NOSE : delicate hints of blueberries and pink roses intertwine with spicy notes, licorice and crusty bread. In the glass, with the passing of the minutes and a few more degrees Celsius, further nuances of black tea and Mediterranean scrub are nourished.

PALATE : on the palate it reveals an elegant structure and a remarkable balance between the roundness given by the 60 months of aging and the freshness of the acidic backbone.
