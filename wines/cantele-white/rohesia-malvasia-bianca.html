White Rohesia Malvasia

From medieval Latin, the name of this wine carries its colour, the chromatic symbolism of its masculine and feminine nature, its delicate ambivalence.

From the single rose, unrepeatable each time, to the elegance that connotes the uniqueness of an entire floral species, a different message travels each time. And that message is not just a matter of colour, it is rather a kind of vocation, just like that of the midday light which – depending on the time of day – degrades the volumes of its shades. And here is a polychromy of the passions. These passions make up the Rohesia line.

A triptych. Red. White. Rose. Three colors of the same rose blossomed in the narrative power of the Rohesia line in three possible variations.

The white rose, as clear as the amazement of certain subdued summer mornings, also holds that secret of dew that has just settled on Malvasia, like the caress that for so long, for a time called night, we have longed to feel, savour.

Tasting notes

COLOR : The golden reflections on the straw-yellow background predict the fragrance and tenacity of this wine.

NOSE : The fruitiness of pineapple and peach is interspersed with the delicate floral of yellow rose, up to the freshness of chamomile.

PALATE : The wine proposes fruit and flower in the mouth; the delicate aroma prolongs the gustatory sensations, giving it a remarkable persistence. The balance between freshness and softness makes this wine dosed in its components, granting the palate a gratification in terms of length and complexity.
